Vegetable Birdseed Pilaf

Description:

A savory vegetarian pilaf of millet with spinach, peas, onions, garlic, and tomatoes flavored with rosemary and wine. "I made this for my non-vegetarian friends and they loved it!" says Lori. "I loved it too. It will be a family staple for sure."

Ingredients:

  • Millet
  • Spinach
  • Peas
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Rosemary
  • Wine

Steps:

  1. Chop spinach, onions,tomatoes, crush garlic
  2. Put all the solid ingredients in a pot
  3. Add enough wine cover all the solid ingredients, then double it.
  4. Bring the wine boil on high heat
  5. Cook the pot, simmering boil on low heat with a lid on for 30 min
