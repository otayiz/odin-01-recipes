Plant-based taco

Description:

A vegetable-rich curry with spinach, sweet potato, eggplant, bell peppers, carrots, onion, garlic, and zucchini, plus garbanzo beans, almonds, and raisins. "Serve this curry over your favorite rice and you're sure to have a tasty meal," says NIBLETS.

"If you have carrot-ginger juice available to you, try using it in place of the orange juice --either one is delicious!"

Ingredients:

  • Spinach
  • Sweet Potato
  • Eggplant
  • Bell peppers
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Zucchini
  • Garbanzo beans
  • Almonds
  • Raisins
  • Orange juice
  • Curry spice

Steps:

  1. Slice and dice spinach, sweet potato, eggplant, bell peppers, onion, garlic, zucchini
  2. Put everything, including garbanzo beans, almonds orange juice and curry spice in a pressure cooker or instant pot
  3. Cook for about 30 minutes after boiling.
  4. Add your favorite taco seasonings to taste
  5. Serve with rice or flatbread and enjoy!
