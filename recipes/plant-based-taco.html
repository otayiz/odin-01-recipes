Plant-based taco

Description:

Healthy and delicious tacos, good for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Cauliflower
  • Black beans
  • Corn
  • Onions
  • Taco seasonings
  • Tortillas

Steps:

  1. Chop cauliflowers, steam them for 5 minutes
  2. Cook black beans and corn using instant pot for 30 minutes
  3. Chop onions, saut√© them with an iron skillet.
  4. Add your favorite taco seasonings to taste
  5. Mix, wrap them in tortillas, and enjoy!
